About

Hi, I am a second-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at Stanford, where I am fortunately to be advised Prof. James Zou and Prof. Ludwig Schmidt. I obtained my B.S. (Honors) degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Zhejiang University.
Previously, I was a research intern at Microsoft Research and DeepSeek AI. Before that, I was also a visiting research student at UCLA Scalable Analytics Institute (ScAi), supervised by Prof. Yizhou Sun and Prof. Wei Wang.
